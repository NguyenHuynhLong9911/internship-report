### Week 4 Objectives

* Learn the core compute services in AWS.
* Practice basic Amazon EC2 operations.
* Understand how Auto Scaling Group helps improve availability and scalability.
* Explore Amazon Lightsail as a simple way to deploy applications.

### Week 4 Achievements

* Understood the basic workflow for creating and managing an EC2 instance.
* Practiced creating snapshots to protect and recover EC2 workloads.
* Installed and tested an application on an EC2 server.
* Learned the main components of Auto Scaling Group, including Launch Template, Target Group, and Load Balancer.
* Understood how Auto Scaling helps applications handle changing traffic and improve availability.
* Explored Amazon Lightsail and compared it with EC2 for simpler application deployment scenarios.
